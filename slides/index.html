The Problem

Educational content is one-size-fits-all. The same textbook is handed to beginners and experts alike.

Beginners get overwhelmed by jargon and drop out.

Advanced learners waste time on material that's too basic.

Learners spend more time searching for the right resource than actually learning.

Our Solution

Upload any document. We rewrite it in real time to match your knowledge level.

📖

AI-Adapted Reading

Content is dynamically rewritten across 5 difficulty levels as you read - from simple analogies to expert-level depth.

🧠

Auto Mind Maps

AI extracts key concepts and relationships from any document into interactive, navigable visualizations.

📝

Smart Quizzes

Auto-generated quizzes validate comprehension and reinforce learning after each reading session.

📈

Knowledge Tracking

Your knowledge profile evolves as you learn, keeping difficulty always in the optimal zone.
